About Me

I started learning to program as a hobby while in my third year of college, and I completed codecademy's 30-hour intro to JS course, as well as their intro to HTML and CSS courses. I decided I really like programming and want to become a Web Developer. I immediately applied as a full-time student in Lambda School's Full-Stack Web Developer and Computer Science program, and was accepted a couple of weeks later.
